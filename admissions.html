Welcome to Prince Jerry Secondary School Admissions

We're grateful that you're considering Prince Jerry Secondary School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, straightforward, and welcoming. We're here to help you every step of the way.

Admissions Process

Our admissions process typically includes the following steps:

  1. Submit an Application Form online or in person.
  2. Attend an Open House or Schedule a School Tour.
  3. Complete an Entrance Examination .
  4. Interview with School Officials .
  5. Receive an Admission Decision.
  6. Complete the Enrollment Process.

Admission Criteria

We admit students based on various factors, including academic performance, character, and an interview . We believe in diversity and inclusivity and welcome students from all backgrounds.
