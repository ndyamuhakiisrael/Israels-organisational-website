Welcome to Prince Jerry Secondary School

Prince Jerry Secondary School is committed to providing high-quality education in a supportive and enriching environment. Our school is dedicated to fostering academic excellence, personal growth, and community involvement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower our students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible citizens. We strive to create a nurturing and inclusive school community that values diversity and encourages students to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational institution known for excellence in academics, character development, and co-curricular activities. We aim to prepare our students to excel in a rapidly changing world while upholding the core values of integrity, respect, and social responsibility.
